Our Security Measures

At Nesba, we employ a comprehensive suite of security measures to safeguard data sourced from Absher, the government portal of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA).

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our platform is encrypted using industry-standard encryption algorithms to ensure confidentiality and integrity.
  • Access Controls: Access to Absher data is strictly controlled and limited to authorized personnel through role-based access controls and stringent authentication mechanisms.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Our platform undergoes real-time monitoring and regular security audits to detect and respond to potential threats and vulnerabilities promptly.
  • Compliance: Nesba adheres to strict regulatory standards, including those set forth by the General Authority for Data Protection (GDPRA) in Saudi Arabia, to ensure compliance with data security and privacy requirements.
  • User Education: We provide comprehensive training programs to our employees and users on best practices for data security, including password hygiene, phishing awareness, and safe browsing habits.
